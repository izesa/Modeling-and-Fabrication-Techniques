# 4. Activity of Day 4: Materials & Fabrication Methods
## 1. Understsnading Materials Through Making.
Understanding Materials helps the user to intergrate the fabrication methods according to what type of material have.
#### This is a fundamental to execeptional design and engineering.

Materials are Not just components as it said in the notes, they are the foundation of what we create. they characterize the durability, functionality and even sustainability of the final prototype. Materials defines what is possible, durable and sustainable. with a low quality material, the final product from frabrication will be unsustainable.

## 2. Material Properties and Selection Strategies.
### 2.1. Key Properties:
#### Strength and Flexibility
the Material must be resistively strong and also easily to be manupulated.
#### Density
This depends on which type of project a given material is requires. with larger projects the density of the material shoul be corresponding as long as for the Small projects.
#### Thermal and Electrical Behavior
The material should ofcourse Favor the electronic activities, for example the material used for manufacturing PCB Board should have a matched electrical behavior with the designing methods.

### 2.2. Key aspect to selection:
#### Intended  Function
When selecting the material to be used first, I have to know which function or project i'm going to work on. This will help the designer to identified what effectively needed.
#### Fabrication Methods
This also determine the type of material to be used
